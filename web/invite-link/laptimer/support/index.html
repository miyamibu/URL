たいむスタンプ Support

たいむスタンプ is an Apple Watch timestamp recorder with an iPhone companion app for reviewing, editing, sharing, and exporting timestamp sessions.

Contact

For support, email mibumiyashita@icloud.com.

Please include your device model, iOS version, watchOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

Supported App Scope

  • Apple Watch timestamp recording with START, STAMP, double-tap session end, and RESET
  • iPhone session list and detail review
  • Session name and memo editing
  • Text sharing
  • XLSX export
  • Session deletion
  • Apple Watch and iPhone sync through WatchConnectivity

Notes

たいむスタンプ does not require sign in, an account, a subscription, or in-app purchases.
